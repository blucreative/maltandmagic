Origin — My Story
20 years ago, got in trouble with the law — a minor issue, but enough to make him leg it to a nearby town to hide it out. He never left. Even through the workplace hardtimes, he never felt it was safe to return home. His home is here now. Nobody asks questions about where someone came from in a town like Tregaw. That suited him fine.
