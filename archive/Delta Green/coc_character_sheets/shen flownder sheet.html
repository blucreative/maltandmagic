Phobia — Fear of Failure
The thing that drove him from London still lives in him. Any situation where he might visibly fail — especially medically — may trigger this phobia while insane.
Note — POW 85 / SAN 85
The highest SAN in the group — Shen is psychologically resilient. POW 85 gives exceptional resistance to magical effects and the deepest Magic Point pool of any investigator (17 MP).
Origin — My Story
Shen was a surgeon in London — a big shot. But after accidentally killing someone on the operating table, he moved south to run from his mistakes and shame. He has never fully shaken it. His fear of failure is not abstract; it has a face, and a date, and a name he doesn't say aloud.
■ Secret — The Margaret Connection
Shen has recently started dating Margaret Barron in secret. She doesn't want it to become known — she fears people in Tregaw will look down on her for moving on after her husband's death. He attends the book club to spend time with her and share her hobby. Nobody else in the group knows.
Personal Description
Broad shoulders, salt-and-pepper hair, tweed suit. The kind of man who looks like he was once very important somewhere else, and has quietly accepted that this is where he is now.
Traits
Quiet and stoic — rarely speaks unless he has something worth saying.

A lover at heart — his stoicism is a shell; there is warmth underneath it.

Not afraid of getting his hands dirty — has always worked with them, and not only in surgery.
Ideology & Beliefs
A man of science, with a specific interest in Biology. He believes in evidence, process, and precision. Faith makes him uncomfortable. Superstition doubly so.
Injuries & Scars
Accidentally killed someone on the operating table in London. It was ruled a complication. He does not agree with that ruling, and he has never forgiven himself.
Significant People
Garry — the local surgeon. A professional connection; Shen's cover in Tregaw is working in some capacity alongside him.

Margaret Barron — owner of the book club, his current partner in secret.

The Librarian — a noted acquaintance, unnamed. (Note: possibly Margaret herself, in her role helping run the library.)
Meaningful Locations
His little cottage — private, small, exactly what he needed. Somewhere nobody knocks unless invited.
Treasured Possessions
His medical kit — not standard-issue. He has procured the best equipment available over the years. It is the one thing he kept from his old life without apology.
Phobias & Manias
Fear of failure — not a vague anxiety. A specific, visceral dread of the moment when his best is not enough and someone pays the price for it.
Encounters with Strange Entities
None recorded. A clean slate — for now.
