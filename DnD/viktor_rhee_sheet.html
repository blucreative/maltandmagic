Character Backstory
I was born and raised in Greywood, groomed and trained from birth as part of the Foresters, like my mother and father and their mother and father before them. It is tradition to only have one child per family — people do have more than one child as they are allowed to, but it is easier to focus solely on one child to train them and get them to live up to their potential. I am an only child.

It has been a generational tradition in Greywood to train and be hunters and protectors, but the reasons why have become more myth and legend, and our decrees and oaths are more ritualistic and of a bygone era. Over time, what we originally started out as hundreds of years ago has evolved to become what is now known as The Foresters. The aim was always to become a member of the monster hunting factions, so I can protect from a greater sphere of influence outside of The Foresters and my immediate home. I believe that the bonds of brotherhood and team can be as strong as blood.
