# The Gloamthorn Court

**Warlock Subclass — D&D 2024 Ruleset**

Your pact binds you to a power born in the Feywild and poisoned by infernal influence. Perhaps your patron was once an archfey of revelry, beauty, twilight, or desire who bartered too deeply with fiends. Perhaps an entire court fell, its songs turned to whispers and its rose gardens grown over with black briars and cinders. Whatever its origin, your patron remains graceful, cunning, and utterly cruel.

The Gloamthorn Court grants gifts of glamour, swiftness, and ruin. Its servants slip between shadows in clouds of ash and petals, speak with honeyed malice, and answer betrayal with pitiless force.

---

## Level 3: Briarstep Blessing

Your patron teaches you to slip between places in a flicker of corrupted glamour. You can cast *Misty Step* without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier, minimum once. You regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

In addition, once on each of your turns, when you reduce a hostile creature to 0 Hit Points, you gain Temporary Hit Points equal to your Charisma modifier plus your Warlock level, minimum 1.

---

## Level 3: Gloamthorn Spells

The magic of your patron ensures you always have certain spells ready; when you reach a Warlock level specified in the Gloamthorn Spells table, you thereafter always have the listed spells prepared.

**Gloamthorn Spells**

| Warlock Level | Spells |
|---|---|
| 3 | Command, Faerie Fire, Misty Step, Suggestion |
| 5 | Blink, Fireball |
| 7 | Fire Shield, Greater Invisibility |
| 9 | Geas, Seeming |

---

## Level 6: Cruel Fortune

Your patron delights in turning disaster into advantage. When you make an ability check or a saving throw, you can use this feature to add 1d10 to your roll. You can do so after seeing the roll but before any of the roll's effects occur.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), but you can use it no more than once per roll. You regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

---

## Level 10: Blighted Resilience

Your body and spirit learn to endure the warped power of your patron. Choose one damage type, other than Force, whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest. You have Resistance to that damage type until you choose a different one with this feature.

---

## Level 14: Cast Through the Briar

Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack roll, you can try to cast the target through your patron's nightmare realm of burning thorns, black roots, and mocking splendour. The target must succeed on a Charisma saving throw against your spell save DC, or the target disappears and hurtles through that realm. The target takes 8d10 Psychic damage if it isn't a Fiend or Fey, and it has the Incapacitated condition until the end of your next turn, when it returns to the space it previously occupied or the nearest unoccupied space.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Pact Magic spell slot (no action required) to restore your use of it.
