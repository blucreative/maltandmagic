## Actions

**Standard Actions:** Attack, Magic, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Ready, Search, Utilize, Opportunity Attack, Grapple, Shove, Improvise, Two-Weapon Fighting, Interact with an Object, Study, Influence

**Bonus Actions:**
- **Pact of the Blade: Bond** — Create a bond with a magic weapon you touch. You can't bond with a weapon someone else is attuned to or another Warlock is bonded with. Until the bond ends, you're proficient with it and can use it as a Spellcasting Focus. Bond ends if you use this Bonus Action again, the weapon is more than 5 ft. away from you for over 1 minute, or you die. A conjured weapon disappears when the bond ends.
- **Pact of the Blade: Conjure** — Conjure a pact weapon in your hand.
- **Second Wind** (2/Long Rest) — Draw on a limited well of stamina to regain 1d10+1 HP. Regain one expended use on finishing a Short Rest.

### Warlock Features
- **Core Warlock Traits** (PHB-2024 p.153)
- **Eldritch Invocations** (PHB-2024 p.153) — Forbidden knowledge granting magical abilities or other benefits; prerequisites must be met to learn an invocation.
- **Pact of the Blade** (PHB-2024) — As a Bonus Action, conjure a pact weapon in hand or bond with a magic weapon you touch (can't bond if another creature is attuned/bonded to it). While bonded, you're proficient with it and can use it as a Spellcasting Focus. Whenever you attack with the bonded weapon, you may use Charisma instead of Strength or Dexterity, and can cause it to deal Necrotic, Psychic, or Radiant damage instead of its normal type. Bond ends under the same conditions listed under Actions above.
- **Pact Magic** (PHB-2024 p.153)

### Feats
- **Two-Weapon Fighting** (PHB-2024 p.210) — *(Fighting Style feat, chosen via the Fighter's Fighting Style feature.)* When you make an extra attack as a result of using a weapon that has the Light property, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of that attack if you aren't already adding it to the damage.
- **Alert** (PHB-2024 p.200) — *(Likely the Origin feat granted by the Versatile Human trait.)* **Initiative Proficiency:** When you roll Initiative, add +2 to the roll. **Initiative Swap:** Immediately after you roll Initiative, you can swap your Initiative with the Initiative of one willing ally in the same combat. You can't make this swap if you or the ally has the Incapacitated condition.
- **Charlatan Ability Score Improvements** (PHB-2024 p.179) — Increase two scores (+2/+1).
- **Weapon Mastery** (PHB-2024 p.91) — Mastery properties applied:

  - **Pistol (Vex)** — Same Vex effect, for Pistols.
  - **Scimitar (Nick)** — When making the extra attack granted by the Light property, you can make it as part of the Attack action instead of a Bonus Action (once per turn only).
- **Skilled** (PHB-2024 p.201) — Gain proficiency in any combination of three skills or tools of your choice. Repeatable — can be taken more than once.
