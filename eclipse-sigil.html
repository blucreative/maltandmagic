The Eclipse Sigil

Wondrous Item · Artifact · Requires Attunement

Eclipse Strain 0 / 10
No strain. Reality holds.

Strain cannot be reduced by any spell, effect, or rest. Tap a pip to add or remove strain.

I. Light — The Celestial Shard
The first light that burned away the Void
Passive
Veil of Truth — You cannot be deceived by magical illusions, glamours, or void-born concealment. You see through any effect that obscures, replaces, or falsifies what is real — understanding that something is false, even if the true form remains uncertain.
Illumination Moderate · 1 Strain
All magical darkness, void concealment, and plane-shifted invisibility within 60 feet fail for 1 minute. Creatures relying on these effects become visible and lose any benefits they granted.
Sundering Revelation Major · 2 Strain Once per Long Rest
One creature within 60 feet is stripped of every illusory, polymorph, or assumed form — forced into its true shape for 1 hour. If the creature's true form is unknown to it, this effect instead deals 6d10 radiant damage as reality asserts itself.
II. Matter — The Abyssal Core
The Unyielding Form. The first material essence.
Passive
Anchor of Form — You cannot be unwillingly moved, teleported, banished, or displaced across planes while the Sigil is active. You are immune to the prone condition from non-physical sources. Forced movement from physical impacts still applies.
Planar Seal Moderate · 1 Strain
Create a Planar Anchor in a 60-foot radius for 10 minutes. Within this area, teleportation, planar travel, summoning, banishment, and dimensional movement all fail unless you permit them. Void seams and planar breaches within the area are suppressed for the duration.
Containment Major · 2 Strain Once per Long Rest
One creature of any size is forced into absolute material fixity — restrained, cannot use teleportation or planar movement, cannot shift form or phase until the start of your next turn. If the target is a void entity or partially non-material, the duration extends to 1 minute, no concentration required. Ignores resistances and immunities to the restrained condition.
III. Energy — The Elemental Seal
The Eternal Flow. The first motion.
Passive
Elemental Resilience — Resistance to fire, cold, lightning, thunder, and acid damage. When you take elemental damage of any type, you may convert it to force damage instead — absorbed into the Sigil's constant motion rather than your body.
Redirect Moderate · 1 Strain Reaction
Intercept one incoming elemental, force, or planar effect targeting yourself or an ally within 30 feet. All damage from that effect is halved for all targets, and the remainder disperses harmlessly as force energy.
Surge Major · 2 Strain Once per Long Rest
Release a single outward pulse. All creatures of your choice within 60 feet — DC 20 Constitution save or take 8d8 force damage and be pushed up to 30 feet in a direction you choose. On a successful save, half damage and not pushed. You and excluded creatures are unaffected.
IV. Thought — The Feywild Amulet
The Dreaming Reality. The first consciousness.
Passive
Warded Mind — Immune to the charmed and frightened conditions. Cannot be detected by divination magic unless you choose to allow it. Any attempt to read, alter, or access your memories or emotions without consent automatically fails.
Probability Shift Moderate · 1 Strain Reaction
Reroll one failed saving throw, attack roll, or ability check — your own or an ally's within 60 feet — and take either result. Can be declared after seeing the original result but before the outcome is determined.
Unwrite Major · 2 Strain Once per Long Rest
One mental, psychic, charm, or memory-based effect currently affecting any number of creatures within 60 feet ends immediately for all affected creatures. Additionally, you may implant a single true statement into one creature's awareness — not a command, simply a fact the creature will know is accurate. The creature retains full autonomy in how they respond to it.
V. Time — The Mortal Keystone
The Measured Existence. The first moment.
Passive
Temporal Immunity — Immune to forced time manipulation — Time Stop, magical aging, temporal stasis, and any effect that removes you from the normal flow of time without consent. You always act in the initiative order regardless of effects that would otherwise prevent it.
Foresight Moderate · 1 Strain
Advantage on all initiative rolls and Dexterity saving throws for 1 hour. Once during this duration, you may declare you knew a particular attack or effect was coming and automatically succeed on the saving throw against it. Must be declared before the roll is made.
Consequence Major · 2 Strain Once per Long Rest
Force a reroll on any single die result that has just occurred — an enemy attack, a failed save, a missed spell, a critical hit — anywhere within 120 feet. You do not choose which result stands. The second result stands regardless. Must be declared before any consequences of the original roll are described.
Resets all once-per-long-rest abilities. Strain is permanent and does not reset.
